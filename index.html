TOKYO BY THE BAY BEACH RESORT

Facilities

REST ROOM

Enjoy a comfortable stay in our air-conditioned restrooms with soft beds, perfect for resting by the sea. We offer large rooms at ₱2,500, medium rooms at ₱1,500, and small rooms at ₱1,000.

COTTAGES

Enjoy the fresh ocean breeze, soft sands, and peaceful waves at our beach, open 24 hours for your convenience. Relax in cottages near the sea, available for ₱400 (day use) and ₱800 (overnight stay). It’s the perfect spot for a day trip or a restful night by the shore.
